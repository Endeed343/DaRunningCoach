About Me


I am a high school student athlete, who enjoys running. I actively participate in both cross country and track. Training during the winter and summer is something that I have found neccessary to success. However, my coach does not offer a training plan for me during the winter and spring, and so I have learned how to make one myself. In order to help other distance runners who want to train during offseason but don't know what to do, I decided to create this website as a fun project, as well as a training tool for all runners everywhere.
